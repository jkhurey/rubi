OSO OSO BASKING IN THE GLOW

Approaching the new album Basking in the Glow by Oso Oso I didn’t have any
expectations of quality or even an idea of what to expect sonically. Little did
I know I was in for an spacey upbeat emo cut album. Expressing
love, isolation, and self-consciousness.With foreshadowing of the romantic crash landing to come
”Got my hand in your hand and my head in the clouds” sings Jade lilitri on the track.
